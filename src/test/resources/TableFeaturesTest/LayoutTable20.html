Metal Fabrication Services


[click to enlarge]
Water Jet
  

Numerical Concepts offers its customers industry leading metal fabrication. From small to large components, we will abrasive water jet cut, flame cut, shear, brake press, weld and sandblast your parts to your specification.

Our machines can handle steel, aluminum, brass, iron, nickel and titanium parts up 310" long and 144" wide. Plus, our Blanchard and surface grinders are some of the largest in the industry.

We also offer a fantastic range of additional services including painting, assembly, engineering, CAD drafting, finite element analysis and finishing. We don't let any of your parts leave our facilities without going through a thorough 100% accurate inspection process that includes hardness and surface roughness testing.

To learn more about our metal fabrication capabilities, see the details below. For information about our other services, please contact an expert at Numerical Concepts today.
